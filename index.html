Select Works

Women in Wage Employment

Military Spending as a Percentage of GDP

The Rising Cost of a Senate Race

In order to create this visual I aggregated several years worth of data from the Federal Election Commission to show the exponential growth of spending in US Senate elections. (Tableau)

I generated a cost per vote metric for each party's US Senate race candidate. The visualization captures the trend of higher cost per vote in non-presidential cycles and the growth of spending since two consequential court decisions in 2010. (Tableau)

The Influencers

Full Republican control of the executive and legislative branches came down to a few battleground Senate races in 2016. This interactive biographical tool allows its users to learn more about the major sources of funding which influenced these critical races. (Javascript, d3)

Beyond Mass Shootings

This visualization calls attention to the daily drip of gun-related fatalities. It scales city names to fatality counts found in the Gun Violence Database and juxtaposes tweets from @NRA, creating a manic frame of information. (Javascript, node, p5)
